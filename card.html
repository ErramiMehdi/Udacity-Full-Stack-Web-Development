Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers consistently rank among the top 3 most popular dogs in the U.S.

  • Origin: Scotland
  • Weight: 65–75 lb (29–34 kg)
  • Lifespan: 10-12 Years
  • Colour: Any shades of gold or cream

Golden Retrievers cannot be used as guard dogs, but they are good as drug dogs at airports and very good as service dogs for the blind (People who cannot see). They also like people, so when they are left alone for too long, they get very sad. Golden retrievers make very good pets because they are friendly and playful.
